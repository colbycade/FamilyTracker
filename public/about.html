    My Family:

    How to Use

    Setting Up Your Family

    One parent of the family should register under the `Login` page where they will receive a family code. Other members of the family will be able use this code to join the family. The parent who registered can designate other members as parents/admins after they join.

    Using the app

    Once you are logged in, you will be able to see your family's to-do list, as well as your personal list. Tasks can be added and marked as complete by by anyone, but only an admin can remove tasks.
