    About

    This is a web application made for CS260

    How to Use

    Setting Up Your Family

    One parent of the family should register under the `Login` page where they will receive a family code. Other members of the family will be able use this code to join the family. The parent who registered can designate other members of the family as admins after they join.

    Using the app

    Once you are logged in, you will be able to see your family's to-do list, as well as your personal list. Tasks can be added and marked as complete by by anyone, but only an admin can remove tasks.
